About Our University

Welcome to MITS

Madanapalle Institute of Technology & Science (MITS) is an institution committed to delivering quality education, encouraging innovation and developing future-ready professionals.

As a Deemed-to-be University, MITS provides an environment that supports academic excellence, research, technological advancement, professional development and holistic student growth.

Through modern learning facilities, experienced faculty, industry interaction and a strong focus on innovation, MITS aims to prepare students to make meaningful contributions to society and industry.
