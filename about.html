Who We Are

Madanapalle Institute of Technology & Science

Madanapalle Institute of Technology & Science, commonly known as MITS, is an institution of higher education located in Andhra Pradesh, India.

MITS is committed to providing quality education that combines academic knowledge, practical learning, research, innovation and professional development.

As a Deemed-to-be University, the institution aims to create a dynamic academic environment where students can develop technical expertise, creativity, leadership abilities and a strong sense of social responsibility.

The university encourages students to go beyond classroom learning by participating in research, technical projects, industry interactions, entrepreneurship, cultural activities and community initiatives.

Our Journey

A Journey of Learning and Growth

MITS has grown as an educational institution with a focus on providing students with opportunities to learn, experiment, innovate and develop their professional capabilities.

Over the years, the institution has continued to strengthen its academic programs, infrastructure, research activities, industry engagement and student development initiatives.

The transition into a Deemed-to-be University represents an important milestone in this journey, creating new opportunities for academic innovation, interdisciplinary education and research.
